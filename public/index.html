Biography

Dongsun Cao is a programmer at UCB Biosciences. His interests include developing SAS programs for generating CDISC-compliant datasets SDTM and ADaM and tables, listings, and figures for NDA submission in the drug development. He is also involved in development of building data visualization tools using JMP scripting language (JSL) and Shiny ® in clinical studies.

Dongsun Cao’s hobby in his spare time is interested in the HVAC and car repairs. He has developed good working knowledge of principle of HVAC and has engaged in the repairing of HVAC system at home and his friends’ houses.
